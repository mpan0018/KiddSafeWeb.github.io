Our Mission

The Kiddsafe project is aimed at reducing Children Injuries in Victorian households

Injuries at home have increased significantly in the current years. It has increased by a huge margin so that the leading cause of deaths is replaced by diseases from injuries in Victorian households. Children are the most affected group with this problem. Kids are at high risk when it comes to home injuries because of the lack of experience in identifying dangerous things. They are the most prone to such injuries.
